Hindi

Hindi , whose standardized form is called Standard Hindi , is a major language of the world and one of the official languages ​​of India . English is the co- official language in India at the central level . It is a standardized form of Hindustani language in which the use of similar and similar words of Sanskrit is more and Arabic-Persian words are less. Hindi is constitutionally the official language of India and the most spoken and understood language of India . According to Ethnologue , Hindi is the third most spoken language in the world. According to the calculations of the World Economic Forum, it is one of the ten most powerful languages ​​in the world. Hindi and its dialects are spoken in various states across India. People in India and other countries also speak, read and write Hindi. Fiji , Mauritius , Guyana , Suriname , Nepal and the United Arab Emirates also have significant numbers of people who use Hindi or its recognized dialects. In February 2019 , Hindi was recognized as the third language of the court in Abu Dhabi .
